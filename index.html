About Us

Wee Community is a not-for-profit organisation that works closely with charities and community organisations.

We understand the different needs; as well as restraints that not-for-profit organisations have compared to other groups. We can provide customisable and tailor made solutions to coincide with your overall aims and ambitions.

We provide day-to-day remote support and scheduled onsite IT services, in addition to ongoing IT advisory and project management services. We also provide IT Support Packages which allows you to customise and tailor support for a broad range of devices.
